Welcome!

This is my personal website, dedicated to projects, poetry, pictures, and reflection.

About me: I am a thinker, a dreamer, a skipper, and a fighter. I believe in childish wonder, and the power of the imagination. My favorite activity is curling up with a good book. Preferably in a recliner, with lots of pillows, bread, and hot chocolate. I like long dresses and big philosophical ideas. Sometimes, I stay up until one AM; debating and challenging the way our world is perceived. My other hobbies include playing violin, and taking long walks.
