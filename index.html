I love programming because it challenges me to solve unique puzzles and lets me create products for others. Besides coding, my favorite activity is probably reading. Preferably in a recliner, with lots of pillows, bread, and hot chocolate. I like long dresses and big philosophical ideas. Sometimes, I stay up until one AM; debating and challenging the way our world is perceived.
